Welcome

Hello, and welcome to Chocs the Fair & Honest fruity chocolate bar.

We are proud to be a FairTrade, earth-friendly product ensuring good wages, a healthy planet, and great chocolate. Scroll down to see more details about our design, price, and FAQ.




Design

The chocolate bar measures 10 x 3 x 2cm approximately. The fruit is scattered over the chocolate in a random pattern. Each fruit topped bar is individually wrapped in paper and tin foil. They can either be sold individually or in large packets of 4, 6, and 10. The larger packets will contain individually wrapped bars.




Price

We are selling our individual bars for £1.40. With the larger packets selling at a higher price. We have chosen these prices as it’s fair for the farmers and keeps the business profitable.
